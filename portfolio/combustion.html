Flame Instabilities In Micro-Combustors


Undergraduate Thesis, Guide: Prof. Sudarshan Kumar, AE, IIT Bombay


Flames in micro-combustors show stunning behaviour when observed through a high speed camera. A ﬂame can have instabilities like rotation or pulsation in a micro-combustor. In the ﬁrst phase of the thesis, experiments were done using LPG-air premixed mixture for diﬀerent equivalence ratios and mixture velocities in a microcombustor. Images and videos of the ﬂames were taken using high-speed camera to observe the very interesting phenomena of rotating and pulsating ﬂames. Regime diagrams, ﬂammability limits and eﬀect of ﬂow velocity and equivalence ratio on ﬂame length and position of ﬂame were studied. In the second phase, the videos would be analyzed to explain the phenomena of rotating and pulsating ﬂames complemented by simulations of ﬂames in micro-combustors.
